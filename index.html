My First Map

How it works:

There are 2 new buttons below: "Fetch markers" and "Clean markers". Pushing "Fetch markers" button you'll get 5 markers based on the position of current Google Map view. If you move the map to another position and press "Fetch markers" button again initial markers will be deleted and new 5 will appear.
After clicking on the marker the InfoWindow will pop up.

It's also possible to just push "Clean markers" button and delete them manually.
